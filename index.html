About

Hello World! I'm Lianna, a Nunatsiavut Inuk from Happy Valley-Goose Bay, Labrador and I'm passionate about merging culture with technology to empower Indigenous communities. Through the IndigiTECH program at the Indigenous Friends Association (IFA), I've gained experience and certifications in Front-End Web Development, UX/UI Design, and Digital Marketing. My Front-End Web Development internship at IFA solidified my commitment to coding accessible and responsive websites.

Keeping my Inuit cultural values at the forefront and combining my background in Community, non-profit, and project management, my passion lies in merging cultural roots with technology, empowering Indigenous communities in this digital age to create visually compelling and user-friendly online spaces that honor tradition while embracing technology. Join me in exploring the intersection of culture, technology, and creativity as we build a digital landscape that celebrates diversity and accessibility.
