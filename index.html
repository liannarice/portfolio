About Lianna

Hello World! I'm Lianna, a Nunatsiavut Inuk originally from Happy Valley-Goose Bay, Labrador, deeply passionate about merging culture with technology to empower Indigenous communities. Through the IndigiTECH program at the Indigenous Friends Association (IFA), I've gained valuable experience and certifications in Front-End Web Development, UX/UI Design, and Digital Marketing.

My journey into web development began with a foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which I've leveraged to code intuitive and visually appealing web experiences. During my Front-End Web Development internship at IFA, I further honed my skills and solidified my commitment to coding accessible and responsive websites that ensure inclusivity and user satisfaction.

Drawing from my background in community, non-profit, and project management, I approach web development with a focus on merging cultural values with technology. My goal is to empower Indigenous communities by creating visually compelling and user-friendly online spaces that honor tradition while embracing modern digital tools.

Join me in exploring the intersection of culture, technology, and creativity as we build a digital landscape that celebrates diversity and accessibility.

Let's connect and discuss how we can bring your ideas to life on the web!
